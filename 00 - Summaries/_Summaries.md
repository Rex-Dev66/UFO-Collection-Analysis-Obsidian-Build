## The collection in one paragraph

This is a structured analysis of **publicly released, declassified U.S. government UAP documents** — mostly Department of War / DoD mission reports, plus State Department cables, CIA cables, FBI case files, and NASA debriefings — routed through the [[AARO Overview|All-Domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO)]]. The reporting is heavily concentrated in **CENTCOM** (~70%), with a smaller domestic (NORTHCOM), Mediterranean (EUCOM), Indo-Pacific, and historical remainder. The vault treats these as **records to organize and assess**, not as proof of any particular conclusion. See [[Collection Overview]] for counts and provenance, and [[Timeline 1944-2026]] for chronology.
